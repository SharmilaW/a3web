About Us

Geographical Information System Specialist

We help clients grow their economies with best-in-class online GIS data tools for investment attraction

When it comes to corporate site selection solutions for online economic development, GeoSolution leads the world. With the use of our package of tools, which includes real estate, demographic, labor force, and industry data, investors may use robust online mapping research to identify the best geographic locations. The software as a service offered by GeoSolution assists areas in attracting new business investment, fostering the growth of current enterprises, and producing jobs.

GeoSolution, founded in 2023 and headquartered in TOronto, Canada, is a technology leader in the development of geographic information systems (GIS)
